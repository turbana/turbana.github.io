 <h1>ShmoopBLTSkrunkleDonk_V</h1>
-<p>To get started you will first want to redeem a hero. To do so, use your twitch channel points to redeem "Adopt a Hero". If you want a specific faction you can type the faction name into the redeem, otherwise leave it blank for a random faction. The possible factions are: Aserai, Battania, Khuzait, Northern Empire, Southern Empire, Sturgia, Vlandia, Western Empire</p>.
-
-<p>Next you will want to choose a class. The possible classes are listed in this documentation under "Classes". Each class has various abilities tied to it's rank. You increase rank by participating in battles. Once you know what you want to play, type "!class <classname>" into chat. Your first class selection is free. Later, if you want to change classes you may do so for a small gold cost</p>.
-
-<p>Each stream you can redeem "Daily XP" and "Daily Gold" channel point redemptions. These will give you extra XP and gold respectively. You will want to redeem those each stream as they are free boosts. You will earn more gold and xp by participating in battles, by kills (and deaths) and by earning achievements</p>.
-
-<p>What do you spend money on? Firstly, by ranking up the tier of your gear; secondly, by hiring retinue to follow you into battle; and lastly by forging new weapons and armor. It's highly recommended to increase your gear tier to at least 4 or 5 before considering purchasing retinue. Forging new gear is an end-game option to gain the most powerful gear. To upgrade your equipment type "!equip" into chat. That will spend gold and raise the tier of your armor and weapons.</p>
